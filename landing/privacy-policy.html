Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026 (v2 — 36 supported retailers)

Esta política también está disponible en español: Política de privacidad

Precio Real is a free, open-source Chrome extension (Manifest V3) that shows the real price history of products on Argentine e-commerce sites. This policy describes, in plain language, what data the extension processes, what it stores, and with whom it is shared. We do not use tracking cookies, we do not require an account, and we have no technical means to identify you personally.

Quick summary

  • We do not collect personally identifiable information. We never ask for your email, name, phone number, or account credentials.
  • We do not sell data to third parties. Ever. There is no way to — we simply don't have it.
  • We do not use analytics inside the extension. The public website (preciorreal.com) uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is aggregate and cookie-free.
  • The code is auditable on GitHub.

What data the extension processes

When you visit a supported site and open a product page, the extension:

  • Reads the public HTML of the product page (URL, title, and current price) using a content script.
  • Sends the canonical URL of the product to our backend (preciorreal.com) to fetch its price history.
  • Receives the price history and displays a badge on the page with a verdict (real price / inflated / neutral).

The extension does not read pages that are not on the list of supported sites. It does not read forms, passwords, payment data, or the contents of your shopping cart.

What is stored and where

In your browser

We use Chrome's storage permission solely to save local extension preferences (for example, whether you dismissed the badge on a particular tab). This data lives on your device and is never sent to any server.

On the Precio Real backend

Our backend (Cloudflare Workers + D1) stores, for each product queried:

  • The canonical URL of the product.
  • The observed price and the date of the observation.
  • Public product metadata (title, site name).

We do not persistently store your IP address, any user identifier, or a per-person browsing history. Requests are anonymous: the backend only sees "someone queried this product", not "you queried this product".

Permissions requested and why

  • storage — Save local extension preferences (e.g., dismissed badges). Used only on your device.
  • activeTab — Read the active tab when you are on a supported site, to detect the product and its price. We do not read inactive or background tabs.
  • Host permissions for the 36 supported e-commerce domains (full list below) — Required for the content script to run only on those sites. The extension is inactive outside that list.

Third-party services

  • Cloudflare (CDN + Workers + D1): hosts the backend and the public website. Cloudflare may log technical request metadata (IP, user agent) for short periods for DDoS protection and operations, according to their own policy. We do not have access to those logs as user data.
  • Cloudflare Web Analytics (only on preciorreal.com, not in the extension): aggregate, cookie-free metrics. Does not identify individual users.
  • Cafecito: only if you choose to tip from the website. The transaction happens on Cafecito's platform; we never receive your payment data.

Data we do NOT use

  • Precise geographic location.
  • Personally identifiable information (name, email, phone, national ID).
  • Payment information, credit cards, or credentials.
  • Browsing history outside the supported sites.
  • Content of messages, forms, or cookies from other sites.
  • Tab list, bookmarks, or browser history.

Children

Precio Real is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from minors. Because we never ask for personal data at any point, there is no information about minors in our systems.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, if the change is material, announce it on the site (preciorreal.com) and in the GitHub repository.
